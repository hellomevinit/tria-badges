Season 3 · VIP Badges
How VIP Badges Work
Trade volume unlocks a badge. The badge guarantees benefits for 30 days. Keep trading to renew it.
The core lifecycle — earn, hold, renew
Step 1 · Earn
Trade hits the threshold
Trade on Hyperliquid or Decibel. The moment your accumulated 30-day trading volume crosses $2M, your badge fires — instantly, not at end of day.

Volume = buys + sells at full notional. A $1M round-trip = $2M of volume.
⚡ Real-time trigger
Important
Benefits are live immediately
The moment the badge activates, the next card transaction earns elevated cashback. Voucher slots unlock right away. No waiting period.
›
Step 2 · Hold
30-day guarantee
From trigger to EOD of day 30, your badge cannot be downgraded — regardless of what you trade (or don't). This is the sacrosanct shelf.

The countdown is shown prominently in the app. Benefits run for the full 30 days.
🔒 No downgrades during window
Inside the window
Upgrade triggers a fresh clock
Keep trading and hit the next milestone ($10M, $30M, $100M) at any point during your window — your badge upgrades instantly to the higher tier and a new 30-day window opens at that tier.
⬆ New clock on upgrade
›
Step 3 · Window end (Day 30 EOD)
One evaluation. Three outcomes.
At 23:59:59 UTC on day 30, the system checks: "How much did you trade since this window opened?"

≥ floor threshold → badge renews, fresh 30 days
≥ lower tier threshold → drops to that tier
< $2M → badge is lost
After renewal
New window opens at $0
Every new window starts with zero accumulated volume. The status chip tells you in real-time whether you're on track to renew again.
Upgrade path within a window

Hit the next milestone anytime — your clock resets

You don't wait for day 30 to upgrade. The moment your accumulated volume crosses the next threshold, the upgrade fires and a fresh 30-day window opens at the new tier. You can skip tiers in a single large trade.

What happens at day 30
🔄
Volume ≥ floor threshold
Badge renews
Same tier continues for another 30 days. New window opens at $0 accumulated volume. Quiet acknowledgment in the app — no big celebration.
⬇
Volume qualifies a lower tier
Badge drops
e.g. VIP-2 holder accumulated $6M — only enough for VIP-1. Badge moves to VIP-1 for 30 days. User sees the gap to regain VIP-2 ($4M more). Can trade back up anytime.
✕
Volume < $2M
Badge is lost
No VIP badge. Screen shows progress toward VIP-1 re-earn. The moment they hit $2M again — badge fires instantly, fresh 30-day window opens.
Key scenarios to know
↕
Upgrade resets everything
On day 8 of VIP-1, user hits $10M → upgrades to VIP-2 with a fresh 30-day window. Old window closes. Prior unredeemed vouchers are forfeited. Already-redeemed vouchers are never clawed back.
⬇
Drop, then trade back up
VIP-2 holder drops to VIP-1 at window end ($6M accumulated, below $10M floor). VIP-1 window opens. They trade back to $10M inside the new VIP-1 window — upgrades to VIP-2 in real-time, new 30-day VIP-2 window opens.
